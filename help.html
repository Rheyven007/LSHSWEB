Welcome to the School Website Help Center

We are here to assist you in navigating our school website and provide you with the necessary information to make the most of your online experience. Whether you have questions, encounter issues, or need guidance, you'll find the answers you're looking for in this help page.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How can I find information about courses/strands offered?
• You can find information about courses/strands offered by our school on our website's Courses page. Simply navigate to the "Courses" tab in the main menu and browse through the list of strands.

2. How do I access my registration portal?
• To access our registration portal, visit our homepage and click on the "Admission" link located in the top navigation menu. If you're facing issues, please refer to the troubleshooting section below.

3. Where can I find the school background?
• Our school background can be found on the "About Us" page. Visit the "About Us" tab in the main menu to access the school background, which includes important details and history.
