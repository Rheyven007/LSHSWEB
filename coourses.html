Welcome to our school's website! We are pleased to offer a wide variety of strands to help our students explore their interests and prepare for their future careers. Our school offers the following strands: STEM, ICT, ABM, HE and HUMSS.




STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) focuses on the application of scientific and mathematical principles to real-world problems. This strand is perfect for students who are interested in pursuing careers in fields such as engineering, computer science, or medicine.

  • Inquiry skills
  • Observstion skills
  • Hypothesis formulation skills
  • Logical thinking

    • ICT (Information and Communications Technology) is a strand that focuses on the use of technology to communicate, manage information, and solve problems. This strand is ideal for students who are interested in careers in fields such as software development, web design, or multimedia arts.

  • Technical skills
  • Information management skills
  • Collaboration
  • Creativity skills
  • Operating skills

They also learn about family dynamics and interpersonal relationships, which are crucial components of building a strong and cohesive family unit.
